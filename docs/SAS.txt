Mini Pharmaceutical Distribution Management System
Software Requirements Specification (SRS) – MVP Version
1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this system is to help a pharmaceutical distributor (agent) manage daily operations including:

Receiving products from pharmaceutical companies
Managing warehouse inventory
Managing pharmacies
Managing sales representatives
Processing orders
Tracking payments
Monitoring commissions
Generating operational reports

The objective is to create a simple and practical system that can operate the business efficiently without the complexity of a full ERP.
