Pharmaceutical Distribution Management System
Software Requirements Specification (SRS)
1. Project Overview
Project Name

Pharmaceutical Distribution Management System

Project Description

The Pharmaceutical Distribution Management System is a centralized platform designed for pharmaceutical distributors (agents) who receive products from multiple pharmaceutical companies, store them in their warehouses, and distribute them to pharmacies through sales representatives.

The system consists of:

1. Head Office Application (Web-Based)

Used by management, warehouse staff, finance staff, and administrators.

2. Sales Representative Application (Mobile App)

Used by field sales representatives to manage customers, check stock availability, and create sales orders.

2. Business Actors
2.1 Pharmaceutical Company

Supplies products to the distributor.

Responsibilities:

Provide products
Define product prices
Define commission rates
Define FOC policies
2.2 Distributor (Agent)

Main business owner using the system.

Responsibilities:

Receive stock from companies
Store products in warehouse
Sell products to pharmacies
Manage receivables and payables
Generate reports
2.3 Sales Representative

Responsibilities:

Visit pharmacies
Promote products
Create sales orders
Manage pharmacy relationships

Restrictions:

Can only access assigned company products
Can only sell products belonging to assigned companies
Cannot approve orders
Can only view available stock
2.4 Pharmacy

Responsibilities:

Purchase products
Make payments
Receive invoices and vouchers
